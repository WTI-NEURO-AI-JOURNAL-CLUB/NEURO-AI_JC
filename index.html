Welcome to the NEURO-AI Journal Club

About

The journal club is a space for researchers, students, and faculty to come together and discuss exciting papers in Neuro-AI. Example topics include:

  • Foundation Models for Neuroscience
  • Neural Dynamics
  • Network-Brain Alignment

    • The discussion will be be led by two presenters each week except in the case of invited guest speakers. If you'd like to present at a future session please sign up here. We meet approximately every other Wednesday at 1pm (lunch at 12.30pm) in 100 College Street, Room 1036 . Our first JC is on Wednesday, February 5th . See you there!
